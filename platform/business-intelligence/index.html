What does GoodData BI do best?

Dashboards and reports made easy for end-users

  • Drag-and-drop UI enables your end-users to create their own visualizations and customize those pre-built by engineers
  • Granular data exploration lets your end-users drill down into dashboards and dig deeper into charts

Business intelligence scalable to many end-users — outstanding

  • Unified governance over end-users and data pipelines enables you to manage and scale your business intelligence from a single platform, without the pains common in other BI tools.

  • Fast and secure scalability to separate groups of users:

    • departments,
    • individual stakeholders,
    • customers,
    • or partners.

Unified metrics across the organization

  • The GoodData’s back-end (data preparation) can be separated from the front-end (visualization UI) and by doing so, then metrics unified and defined in a single semantic data model can be connected to different visualization tools.
  • Context-aware metrics built in GoodData BI can be created once, reused time and time again, and aren’t easily broken.
